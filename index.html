About Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission) was launched on October 2nd, 2014 by the Hon'ble Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi, with the goal of making India clean and free of open defecation.

The mission emphasizes building toilets, proper waste disposal, and encouraging citizens to take responsibility for the cleanliness of their surroundings. It's a nationwide movement that involves schools, colleges, local bodies, and individuals working together for a cleaner, greener India.

About Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission) is a nationwide campaign launched by the Government of India on 2nd October 2014 to eliminate open defecation, improve solid waste management, and promote cleanliness across cities and villages in India.

It is inspired by Mahatma Gandhi's vision of a clean and hygienic India. The mission encourages active participation from all citizens, schools, municipalities, and NGOs to make India clean and green.
